EvoSynth: Get Involved!

Communicate

  • Subscribe to the evosynth-devel mailing list. The central place to ask questions, share patches and discuss the future development of evosynth.
  • file bugreports and feature-requests

Patches are welcome

Want to submit a patch? Either create a merge request at github / gitorious or send the patch to the evosynth-devel mailing list.
